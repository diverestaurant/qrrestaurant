# Staff Training Plan

Status: Planned; no training performed  
Last updated: 2026-07-20

## Format

- Role-specific short sessions using Staging/controlled test table.
- Demonstration → guided practice → unassisted task → exception drill.
- Quick-reference guides with screenshots are created only after UI stabilizes.
- Attendance, trainer, build/environment and competence result recorded.

## Common staff module

- Sign in/out, Branch/role context and session expiry.
- Recognize Offline/Reconnecting/Stale/Conflict/Unknown Outcome.
- Do not retry order/payment blindly; use safe status/retry path.
- Privacy: do not copy tokens, payment references or customer notes into chat/logs.
- Incident/support contact and manual fallback.

## Manager/Admin

- Restaurant/Branch/table/station/menu setup.
- QR/Session Join Code rotation and scope.
- Staff invite/disable/role preview.
- Sold-out/restore and immediate menu-change impact.
- Discount/cancellation/force-close reasons and audit.
- Reports/timezone/business date/audit.
- Unsupported Deferred features and correction SOP.

Competence tasks: configure synthetic item, rotate QR, disable staff, inspect audit and run report without crossing Branch scope.

## Kitchen

- Device/station setup, audio/fullscreen/wake state.
- Read table/quantity/variant/modifier/notes.
- Accept → Preparing → Ready.
- Partial order/station/expo/unassigned queue.
- Multi-device conflict and recently completed.
- Offline/reconnect/last sync/manual fallback.

Competence task: process a mixed-station order and reconnect without duplicate state changes.

## Waiter

- Floor/status/service inbox.
- Open table and help customers use current Join Code.
- Assisted ordering and staff attribution.
- Partial serving and conflict recovery.
- Bill handoff, eligibility to clear table and permission limits.

Competence task: open, assisted-order, resolve request, serve and hand off a test table.

## Cashier

- Checkout queue and authoritative bill.
- Authorized discount/reason.
- Cash/change and other manual tenders.
- Multi-tender outstanding balance; no Split Bill by item/person.
- Failed/unknown payment and duplicate prevention.
- PAID/close recovery and receipt reprint.
- Daily reconciliation and escalation.

Competence task: complete two-tender checkout, simulate lost response and close without double payment.

## Pilot readiness

- All active Pilot staff complete role task or have supervised coverage.
- Leads can perform outage/recovery procedure.
- Staff understand refunds/void/reopen/transfer/split are Deferred.
- Training issues are categorized Must Fix/Accepted Risk/Future.

Training content cannot be finalized until implementation screenshots, approved business policies and staff roles exist.
